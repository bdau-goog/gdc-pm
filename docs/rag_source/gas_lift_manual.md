# Gas Lift Compressor Maintenance Manual

## valve_failure
Check valve failure causes immediate loss of lift gas injection pressure.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Isolate and Replace Check Valve: Shut down compressor, swap check valve disk. (Cost: $3,200, Time: 4 hours)
2. Full Compressor Overhaul: Pull unit offline, inspect all valves and internal components. (Cost: $25,000, Time: 3 days)

## thermal_runaway
Cylinder jacket cooling failure causes discharge temperature to climb above 230°F.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Reduce Compressor Speed 20%: Lowers thermal load immediately while cooling is investigated. (Cost: $0, Time: Instant)
2. Clean Heat Exchanger and Inspect Cooling Circuit: Flush fouling, restore cooling water flow. (Cost: $1,800, Time: 6 hours)

## bearing_wear
Journal bearing wear produces rising frame vibration signature — crankshaft imbalance risk.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Schedule Planned Bearing Replacement: Monitor vibration closely, plan downtime window. (Cost: $12,000, Time: 8 hours)
2. Emergency Shutdown and Immediate Inspection: Stop unit now to prevent seizure and compressor loss. (Cost: $85,000, Time: 2 days)
