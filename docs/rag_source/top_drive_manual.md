# Top Drive Maintenance Manual

## gearbox_bearing_spalling
Gearbox bearing race fatigue produces an increasing vibration signature — seizure risk in 4–12 hours.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Reduce Top Drive RPM 15% and Monitor Vibration Trend: Buy time for orderly shutdown planning. (Cost: $0, Time: Instant)
2. Pull Top Drive for Emergency Bearing Replacement: Stop drilling, rig up crane, pull unit, replace bearing set. (Cost: $45,000, Time: 12 hours)

## hydraulic_leak
Hydraulic fluid loss reduces top drive torque capacity — loss of directional control risk.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Inspect and Repair Hydraulic Line Leak: Locate source, tighten or replace fitting/hose, top up fluid reservoir. (Cost: $800, Time: 2 hours)
2. Shutdown and Full Hydraulic System Inspection: Stop drilling, bleed system, pressure test all circuits, replace seals. (Cost: $8,000, Time: 6 hours)
