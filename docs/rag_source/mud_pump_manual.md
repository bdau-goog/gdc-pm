# Triplex Mud Pump Maintenance Manual

## pulsation_dampener_failure
Pulsation dampener bladder rupture creates extreme pressure hammer — immediate pipe-rupture risk.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Emergency Shutdown and Area Evacuation: Stop all drilling immediately, evacuate pump room, inspect system. (Cost: $0, Time: Immediate)
2. Replace Dampener Bladder and Resume Operations: With pump offline, replace bladder assembly and pressure test. (Cost: $8,500, Time: 6 hours)

## valve_washout
Valve seat erosion causes discharge pressure to slowly decline as the valve leaks past seat.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Reduce Pump Speed and Monitor: Lower stroke rate, track differential pressure trend, plan fluid end rebuild. (Cost: $0, Time: Instant)
2. Rebuild Fluid End — Replace Valves and Seats: Pull fluid end, replace all valves, reseat, pressure test. (Cost: $18,000, Time: 8 hours)

## piston_seal_wear
Liner seal degradation causes gradual fluid end temperature rise and slight pressure loss.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Schedule Planned Seal Replacement Window: Low urgency — coordinate a 24-hour maintenance window. (Cost: $4,500, Time: 8 hours planned)
2. Emergency Seal Replacement: Stop pump immediately, replace liner seals and piston cups now. (Cost: $4,500, Time: 4 hours)
