# Electrical Submersible Pump (ESP) Maintenance Manual

## Gas Lock
Gas entrainment in the fluid stream can cause the ESP to lock up.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Reduce VFD Frequency 15%: Lowers intake pressure requirements, allowing gas to pass. (Cost: $0, Time: Instant)
2. Open Casing Valve: Bleeds gas from the annulus. (Cost: $500, Time: 2 hours)

## Sand Ingress
Sand from the formation can erode the impeller stages.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Inject Chemical Sand Consolidator: (Cost: $5,000, Time: 4 hours)
2. Schedule Full Workover: Pull the pump and replace eroded stages. (Cost: $85,000, Time: 3 days)

## Motor Over-Temp
Motor winding temperature climbing above 280°F.
**Resolution Options:**
1. Reduce Pump Frequency 15%: Reduces load and heat generation. (Cost: $0, Time: Instant)
2. Increase Choke Size: Increases flow rate to cool the motor. (Cost: $0, Time: Instant)
